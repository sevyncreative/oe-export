Common questions

Frequently asked

What areas does OE Services serve?
OE Services is licensed in Nevada, California and Arizona. We serve all of Clark County NV (Las Vegas, Henderson, North Las Vegas, Boulder City), Northern California within 1.5 hours of Richmond (Oakland, Berkeley, San Jose, Sacramento), Southern California from Pasadena, San Diego and Murrieta hubs, and the Phoenix metro area from Mesa AZ.
How much does an electrical panel upgrade cost?
A typical 200-amp residential panel upgrade ranges from $2,200 to $4,800 installed, including permits and inspection. Pricing varies by region, panel location, and whether a service mast or meter relocation is needed. Use our free online estimator for an instant price specific to your home.
Does OE Services help with rebates and tax credits?
Yes. We handle all rebate paperwork on your behalf, including federal IRA tax credits (25C / 25D), state programs (CA TECH Clean California, NV Energy, AZ APS/SRP), and local utility rebates. Our estimator automatically shows which rebates you qualify for based on your project.
Is OE Services licensed and insured?
Yes. OE Services holds licenses #0093240 and #0087180 in Nevada, #1057069 and #1124897 in California, and ROC #354397 in Arizona. We carry full liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage.
How long has OE Services been in business?
OE Services has over 18 years of experience delivering electrical and general contracting services for residential, commercial, and new construction projects.
Can I get an estimate without a salesperson coming to my house?
Yes. Our online estimators give you a real, ranged price in under 2 minutes — no salesperson, no pressure. If you like the range, we'll schedule a free in-person verification and lock in your final number.
