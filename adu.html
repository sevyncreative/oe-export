⚠️ IMPORTANT ASSUMPTIONS — READ BEFORE CONTINUING
Build-out pricing assumes an existing, permitted shell space is already in place (walls up, roof on, concrete slab, utilities stubbed in). If you are starting from bare land or a new structure, select "New Ground-Up Construction" below — that option calculates by gross square footage for the full shell.

All commercial estimates exclude: land cost, architectural drawings (quoted separately at consultation), FF&E (furniture, fixtures & equipment), branding, signage, health department inspection fees, and liquor/entertainment licensing. Final costs vary significantly by jurisdiction and site — this is a planning ballpark only.

📋 COMMERCIAL ESTIMATE — WHAT'S INCLUDED & EXCLUDED
Included in this estimate: build-out labor and materials for the space type selected, framing, MEP rough-in and trim-out, flooring, ceiling, lighting, paint, and trade-specific requirements (kitchen rough-in, plumbing stations, electrical panels, etc.).
